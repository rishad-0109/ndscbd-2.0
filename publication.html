Audri 2025

Audri, the annual science publication of the Notre Dame Science Club, is a vibrant reflection of the curiosity, passion, and intellectual spirit of the science-minded students of Notre Dame College. More than a collection of student writings, it serves as a platform where research, analysis, and innovative ideas extend beyond the classroom to reach a wider audience. Celebrating science as a continuous journey of discovery, Audri encourages critical thinking, creativity, and free expression. Through its pages, it inspires curiosity, broadens perspectives, and reveals the enduring beauty of science while making knowledge engaging and accessible to all.
