AUDRI 2023-2024

Audri, the annual science publication of the Notre Dame Science Club, is a vibrant reflection of the curiosity, passion, and intellectual spirit of the science-minded students of Notre Dame College. More than a collection of student writings, it serves as a platform where research, analysis, and innovative ideas extend beyond the classroom to reach a wider audience. Celebrating science as a continuous journey of discovery, Audri encourages critical thinking, creativity, and free expression.
