WHY YOU SHOULD JOIN NDSC?

Historical Legacy & Lead in Science Education :

Notre Dame Science Club holds the opportunity to be part of a club with a long-standing tradition of excellence in science education and contribute to the legacy of one of the most influential science communities in the region that has pioneered scientific clubs and activities in Bangladesh

Research Development & Expeimental Skill :

Notre Dame Science Club often involves in various scientific research, experiments and projects where you can learn new things and ideas to strengthen your critical thinking, problem solving skills. To help you more with that we have a dedicated “Research & Development Wing” where you will meet more people who shares the same interests as you

Engagement in Competitive Competitions

Notre Dame Science Club always gives the advisability to challenge yourself by participating in science Olympiads and competitions to represent your school and country on national and international platforms and at the same time paves the way to gain self-recognition through your achievements by improving your skills and knowledge through the competitive spirit

Building Leadership & Collaboration Skills

Notre Dame Science Club always encourages to solve problems and achieve common goals with teamwork and taking leadership roles in organizing projects, competitions, and activities in order to gain experience, learning to manage responsibilities, coordinate tasks, and delegate work effectively for future leadership roles in both academic and professional environments

Proficiency in Social Networking & Discipline :

Notre Dame Science Club prefers to build connections with like-minded individuals, mentors, and professionals in the science field by attending networking events, workshops, and conferences to enhance your professional profile and develop a professional identity in the community as well as getting career guidance from experienced scientists and faculty members

Commitment to Social Awareness and Service :

Notre Dame Science Club is deeply committed to actively contribute to the community for developing through science-driven projects and be a part of initiatives that use science for social good, like health, education, and environment to promote awareness on important global issues like climate change, health crises, and attain not only sustainability but also develop a sense of responsibility of the young generation towards society and the environment
